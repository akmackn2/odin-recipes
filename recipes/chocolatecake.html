The Necessary Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of White Sugar.
  • 1.75 cups of All Purpose Flour.
  • 3/4 teaspoons of Unsweetened Cocoa Powder.
  • 1.5 teaspoons of Baking Soda.
  • 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 eggs (Large)
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1/2 Cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 2 teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup of Boiling Water

The Directions to Completion:

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit or 175 degrees Celcius.
  2. Grease & Flour two 9 inch round cake pans.
  3. In a bowl, stir all together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  4. In the bowl, add eggs, milk, oil, vanilla, and mix it for total of 3 minutes with an electric mixer. Stir it in boiling water by hand.
  5. In the prepared pans, pour it out evenly.
  6. Next, place the prepared pans in the oven for 30-35 minutes.
  7. At the suggested time, place a toothpick in the center of the cake and ensure it comes out to clear.
  8. Have the cake cool for 10 minutes prior to removing it from the pan.
  9. While cakes cool, make frosting: Cream butter with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Stir in confectioners' sugar and cocoa alternately with milk and vanilla. Beat to a smooth spreading consistency.
  10. Split the layers of the cake horizontally.
  11. Cover the top of each layer with frosting, then stack them on the plate you would like for the cake to be served with.
  12. For any remaining frosting, continue to just frost up the cake.
  13. Finally, enjoy the amazing Back-of-the-Box Hershey's Chocolate Cake!
