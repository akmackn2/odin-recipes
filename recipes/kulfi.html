The Necessary Ingredients:

  • 1.25 Cups of Evaporated Milk
  • 1.25 Cups of Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 16 oz. Container of Frozen Whipped Topping (Whipped Cream)
  • 4 Slices of White Bread to Be Torn Into Chunks of Pieces
  • 1/2 tsp. of Grand Cardamom

The Directions to Completion:

  1. Blend the Evaporated Milk, Sweetened Condensed Milk, and Frozen Whipped Topping in a blender.
  2. Blend in pieces of bread until smooth.
  3. After blending, pour that mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish or two plastic ice cube trays.
  4. Sprinkle the poured mixture with the Grand Cardamom and freeze for at least 8 hours.
